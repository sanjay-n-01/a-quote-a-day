Submit a Quote

Thank you for contributing to A Quote A Day! ✨ This page explains how to submit your quote correctly using a pull request.

1. Filename (Required)

Your quote will appear on the site only if the filename starts with a date.

Files without a date in the filename are ignored by the quote-processing system.
YYYY-MM-DD_name_department.md

Example:

2026-02-04_meenz_cyber-security.md

2. File Content Format

Copy and paste the following template into your file and fill it in. 

---
quote:
author:
contributor:
department:
source:
tags:
about:
---
